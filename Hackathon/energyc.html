Pune Smart City: Energy Consumption

Pune Smart City is actively working to promote energy efficiency and reduce energy consumption.


Key Initiatives

  • **Smart Street Lighting:**
    • LED Lights
    • Intelligent Control Systems
  • **Energy-Efficient Buildings:**
    • Green Building Standards
    • Retrofitting Existing Buildings
  • **Renewable Energy:**
    • Solar Power
    • Wind Energy
  • **Smart Grid:**
    • Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI)
    • Demand Response Programs
  • **Energy Efficiency Campaigns:**
    • Public Awareness
    • Incentives

Benefits of Smart Energy Initiatives

  • Reduced energy costs
  • Environmental sustainability
  • Improved energy security
  • Economic growth

Challenges and Future Directions

  • Infrastructure
  • Public participation
  • Policy implementation
  • Technological advancements


Pune Smart City's efforts in energy management are crucial for creating a sustainable and resilient city.
