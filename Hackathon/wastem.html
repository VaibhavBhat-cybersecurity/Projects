Pune Smart City: Waste Management

Pune Smart City has implemented innovative solutions to address the challenges of waste management in the city.



Key Initiatives

  • **Segregation at Source:**
    • Promoting awareness and encouraging citizens to segregate waste at source.
    • Providing guidelines and bins for proper segregation.
  • **Smart Waste Collection:**
    • GPS Tracking
    • RFID Technology
    • IoT Sensors
  • **Waste Processing and Recycling:**
    • Waste-to-Energy Plants
    • Recycling Facilities
    • Composting
  • **Swachh Bharat Abhiyan:**
    • Active participation in the government's Clean India Mission.
  • **Public Awareness Campaigns:**
    • Conducting campaigns to educate citizens.
    • Encouraging citizen participation.

Benefits of Smart Waste Management

  • Reduced pollution
  • Resource conservation
  • Improved sanitation
  • Sustainable development

Challenges and Future Directions

  • Public participation
  • Infrastructure development
  • Technology adoption
  • Community involvement

Pune Smart City's efforts in waste management are crucial for creating a cleaner, greener, and more sustainable city.
