Pune: The Queen of the Deccan



Overview

Pune, a city in the western Indian state of Maharashtra, is known for its rich history, cultural heritage, and educational institutions. It was once the capital of the Maratha Empire and is now a bustling metropolis.



Historical Significance

Pune played a pivotal role in the Maratha Empire, serving as its capital for a significant period. It witnessed the rise and fall of the Maratha rulers and has numerous historical landmarks.

  • Shaniwar Wada
  • Aga Khan Palace
  • Shinde Chhatri
  • Vishrambaug Wada

Educational Hub

Pune is renowned as an educational hub, often referred to as the "Oxford of the East." It houses numerous prestigious universities and colleges.

  • Savitribai Phule Pune University

Tourist Attractions

Pune offers a variety of tourist attractions, from historical sites to natural beauty.

  • Shravanabelagola
  • Bhimashankar_Temple
  • Lonavala
  • Khadakwasla Dam
